OBJECTIFS ET CONTENU DU COURS

GENERAL OBJECTIVE OF THE COURSE

The aim of the course is to provide participants with an understanding of the theoretical and practical framework associated with dry needling.

COURSE CONTENT

The course will cover the following topics:

  • Indications and contraindication of dry needling
  • Safe dry needling technique
  • Proper dosing associated with dry needling
  • Physiology associated with dry needling analgesia
  • Physiology associated with dry needling mediated tissue repair
  • Mechanical and electric stimulation associated with dry needling
  • Dry needling of Osteoarthritis of the knee – demonstration and practice

LEARNING OUTCOMES

  • Participants will be able to verbalize 3 indications and contraindications of dry needling
  • Students will verbalize and demonstrate safe dry needling technique
  • Students will state 3 variables associated with proper dry needling dosage
  • Students will demonstrate proper manual and electric stimulation associated with dry needling
  • Students will demonstrate ability to perform a general dry needling protocol for knee osteoarthritis in a manner that is safe and evidence-based
  • Students will describe two mechanisms associated with dry needling mediated pain relief and tissue repair
