COURSE DESCRIPTION

Cette formation est conçue pour les étudiants en physiothérapie et se concentre sur la technique du Dry Needling, une approche thérapeutique utilisant des aiguilles fines pour traiter les douleurs musculo-squelettiques.

TARGET AUDIENCE

This course is designed for second-year students in physiotherapy, with a cohort of approximately 118 students, divided into 4 groups of around 24/26 people. The duration of each session per group is limited to 1.5 hours, as outlined in the attached program included in the mobility agreement.

GENERAL OBJECTIVE OF THE COURSE

The aim of the course is to provide participants with an understanding of the theoretical and practical framework associated with dry needling.
